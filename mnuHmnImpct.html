Human Impact

GLOBAL WARMING
Ozone depletion at the North and South Poles means stronger ultraviolet rays will be hitting and harming the tundra. The melting of the permafrost is a result of the stronger ultraviolet rays. Global warming could completely change the landscape and population of the Tundra.
